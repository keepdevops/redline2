1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Email address, name, and password (encrypted)
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our service, including processing jobs and file operations
  • Payment Information: Billing details processed securely through Stripe (we do not store full credit card numbers)
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, and cookies

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the VarioSync service
  • Process your payments and manage subscriptions
  • Send service-related notifications and updates
  • Improve and optimize our service
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Data Storage and Security

We take data security seriously:

  • All data is encrypted in transit using SSL/TLS
  • Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text
  • User files are stored in secure cloud storage (Wasabi S3)
  • Authentication is managed through Supabase with JWT tokens
  • Payment processing is handled by Stripe (PCI DSS compliant)

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with:

  • Service Providers: Supabase (authentication), Stripe (payments), Wasabi (file storage), Modal (processing)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights

5. Your Data Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and data
  • Export your data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Maintain your session and authentication
  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze service usage and performance

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. After account deletion:

  • User files are deleted within 30 days
  • Account data is anonymized for billing records (required by law)
  • Usage logs are deleted within 90 days

8. Children's Privacy

VarioSync is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

10. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or through the service.

11. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or to exercise your data rights, please contact us through the support channels provided in the application.
